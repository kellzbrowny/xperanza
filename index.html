REGISTER
  • WE Scout Talents - The Xperanza got talent south-South season one being the maiden edition is set to scout young untapped talents from around the south-south region and bringing them to limelight
  • Build Brand Ambasedors - The effect of musical Super stars cannot be over emphasize on Brand marketing therefor this event promises to recruit suitable artist into becoming Influential Brand Marketers and music super stars

ENTRY METHOD
Each participant is expected to do a one minute video recording on Instagram and TAG @Xperanzaworld

AUDITIONS
Audition will take effect from August 21 to August 28

Competition Stages

 August 21-25

Stage One

50 Persons will be selected in stage one this will be based on the number of video likes and performance. That is the standard of the judges and number of video likes .

August 26-28

Stage Two

10 Person will be selected in this stage. This is strictly based on the standard of the judges. 

August 30

Stage Three

This is the Final Stage of the Show . Three persons will be qualified to compete at this stage .this stage will be a live broadcast on Air at the studio of Sparkling 92.3 Fm calabar. This Stage being the gran finale of the event , There will be live instrumentals and the judges will be live on air too. Here the winner of the maiden edition of the Xperanza got talent show will be announced.
